Pizza

Ingredients

POOLISH:
  • 300 ml Lukewarm Water
  • 5 g Dry Yeast
  • 5 g Honey
  • 300 g 00 Flour
DOUGH:
  • 700 ml Lukewarm Water
  • 40 g Salt
  • 1,250 g 00 Flour

Steps

POOLISH:
  1. Mix together all ingredients
  2. Cover and set at room temperature for 1-hour
  3. After rest place overnight in fridge
DOUGH:
  1. Together with water in a large bowl, add the Poolish, 1/2 flour and all of the salt
  2. Mix well, add remaining flour to bowl and mix all well---dough will be sticky but together.
  3. Hand knead for 5-10 mins; cover with bowl and let rest for 15-mins.
  4. Remove bowl and keeping top on top always, using bench scraper work dough with hands kinda like a big ball, with fingers underneath lifting dough off board, repeat.
  5. Coat a little olive oil in mixing bowl then gently place dough in bowl; allow to rest/rise for 1-hour with cover.
  6. Remove dough onto work board; coat your hands with olive oil and pat on top of the dough.
  7. Make 3 equal cuts across the dough. Take one end and shape ball with one hand, cut dough with other.
  8. This method makes nice round balls.
  9. Coat a sheet pan with Semolina flour and place dough balls about 2-3 fingers apart; little oil on top dough balls and cover with plastic wrap.
  10. Rest at room temp for 2-hours.
Notes:

I ALWAYS use my Kitchen Aid Stand Mixer vs. hand-kneading. My dough comes out perfect every time.
