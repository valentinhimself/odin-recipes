Creamy Roasted Garlic Butternut Squash Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cups cubed butternut squash
  • 6-10 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary
  • crushed red pepper flakes
  • kosher salt and black pepper
  • 8 slices prosciutto
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound long or short cut pasta
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped sage
  • 1/2 cup shredded gouda cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. First, toss the squash and garlic with lots of fresh thyme, rosemary, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
  2. Arrange the prosciutto around the squash and roast for a bit.Of course, if you don’t eat meat, you can easily omit the prosciutto or add sun-dried tomatoes
  3. Once the squash has finished roasting, puree it with the garlic and ricotta cheese until smooth. You can do this in a food processor or blender.
  4. Next, boil the pasta, but save some of the water for thinning sauce.
  5. Now melt the butter in a skillet with sage. Next, add the squash purée and a splash of the cooking water. Stir in some gouda cheese, add the pasta, and toss, toss, toss. Taste and season as needed. And well…you’re done.
  6. Serve topped with extra parmesan and that crispy prosciutto.
